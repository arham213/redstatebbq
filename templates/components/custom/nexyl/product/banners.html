First Taste to Finish

Creamy and tangy up front, a little peppery, then a horseradish kick on the finish. True Alabama white-sauce character vinegar and spice cutting straight through brisket, prime rib, and anything else rich and smoked.

Put it on a Smoked Turkey Sandwich

Nobody puts white sauce on turkey. Do it anyway it's the move our regulars don't know to ask for. Stack it up:

1: Toasted sourdough 2: Smoked turkey + crisp bacon 3: Provolone, lettuce, tomato 4: A good pour of Alabama Show Horse
